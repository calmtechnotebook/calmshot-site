What makes it different

Designed to create,
not to complicate.

Cinematic blur
Apple's native Cinematic Video API creates real-time background blur. No green screen, no post-production. Just you, in focus.
Teleprompter by the lens
Your script scrolls just below the camera lens. Adjustable speed, natural eye contact. No second screen needed.
Calm pause before filming
Box breathing (4-4-4-4) with gentle haptics. Take a moment to center yourself before you press record.
Privacy-first
No accounts. No tracking. No data collected. Everything stays on your device. Your videos go straight to Photos.
Manual controls
Aperture slider for bokeh depth. Exposure slider for brightness. Tap-to-focus with AE/AF lock. You're in control.
One-time purchase
$14.99 and it's yours. No subscription, no in-app purchases, no premium tiers. Every feature, forever.

"The best tools are the ones that disappear when you're using them."

CalmShot is built on the principles of Calm Tech Notebook — a design philosophy where tools work at the periphery of your attention, not in competition with it. No engagement metrics. No social features. No complexity for complexity's sake. Just a camera that helps you film with intention.

01
Local-first architecture
Your scripts, your settings, your videos — all stored locally on your iPhone. No cloud sync required, no server dependency, no account to create.
02
Configure before filming
All shooting settings live in a dedicated section. When you're in the viewfinder, there's nothing between you and your content except the record button.
03
Native performance
Built entirely on Apple's Cinematic Video API. No third-party frameworks, no custom shaders, no compromises. The blur and stabilization you see is Apple-grade quality.
04
Intentional by design
Filming is not a race. CalmShot encourages preparation, calm, and deliberate engagement with your content. A breathing exercise before you record, not a countdown to urgency.
