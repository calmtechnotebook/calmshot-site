Getting Started

What iPhone do I need?

CalmShot requires an iPhone 11 or newer running iOS 26. The Cinematic background blur feature works on all supported devices, as it uses Apple's native Cinematic Video API.

Is there a subscription?

No. CalmShot is a one-time purchase at $14.99. You pay once and own it forever, including all future updates. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no surprise charges.

Does it work offline?

Completely. CalmShot requires no internet connection — not to launch, not to record, not to save. Everything happens on your device.

Camera & Recording

What is the background blur?

CalmShot uses Apple's native Cinematic Video API to create a real bokeh effect — the same technology used in Apple's Camera app. This is not a filter or a simulation. It produces a genuine depth-of-field effect in real time.

Use the Aperture slider to control the intensity of the blur.

What resolution does CalmShot record in?

CalmShot records in 9:16 vertical format and automatically selects the best resolution supported by your device — up to 4K. You can choose your preferred resolution in Settings.

Where are my videos saved?

Every recording is saved automatically to your iPhone's Photos library as soon as you stop recording. There is no in-app library — CalmShot saves directly to Photos and gets out of the way.

What are the Aperture and Expo sliders?

The Aperture slider (left) controls the depth of field — how blurred the background is. Slide down for more blur, up for less.

The Expo slider (right) controls the exposure — how bright or dark the image is. Useful when your environment is too bright or too dim.

How does tap-to-focus work?

Tap anywhere on the viewfinder to set the focus and exposure point. A circle appears to confirm. For a long press (0.6s), the focus and exposure lock — a square with a lock icon confirms this. Tap elsewhere to unlock.

Teleprompter

How do I use the teleprompter?

Go to the Script tab, create a new script, and type or paste your text. Back on the Camera tab, select your script from the "Active Script" menu. The text will appear just below the lens as an overlay while you film.

The script scrolls automatically when you start recording. Adjust the speed with the Vitesse slider.

Can I scroll the script manually?

Yes. Drag up or down on the script overlay to navigate forward or backward through your script at any time.

How many scripts can I save?

Up to 10 scripts. You can also export any script as a .txt file to the Files app for backup or sharing.

Privacy & Data

What data does CalmShot collect?

Nothing. CalmShot collects zero data. No analytics, no crash reports, no usage tracking. The app has no network access. Your scripts, preferences, and videos never leave your device.

Do I need to create an account?

No account, no sign-in, no email address. You download the app and use it immediately.
